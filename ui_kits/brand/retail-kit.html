The GHXSTSHIP product system — reusable image templates for apparel, equipment, and accessories, organized into three lines: Sitewear, Streetwear, and Performancewear. Every tile is a template; real product photography drops straight in.

The Template System

One Shot, Three Categories.

Every product photo follows the same frame: 1:1 crop, line-colorway ground, Ben-Day halftone, faint skull watermark (bottom-right, 10%), category badge (top-left), and colorway swatches (bottom-left). SKU format: GX-[LINE]-[CAT]-NNN.

Template A

Apparel

  • Flat-lay or ghost-mannequin, front + back
  • 1:1 hero, 4:5 detail crop
  • Colorway swatches required
  • Size run in product body, not image

Template B

Equipment

  • 3/4 hero on seamless ground
  • Scale reference + in-use shot
  • Spec callouts as overlay labels
  • Durability / certification badge

Template C

Accessories

  • Tight 1:1 macro, single hero
  • Pairs/sets shown together
  • Material + dimension in body
  • Optional lifestyle alt
