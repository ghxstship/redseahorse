Answers

Solutions FAQ.

Can GHXSTSHIP act as the general contractor for an experiential or entertainment project?+

Yes. GHXSTSHIP operates as the general contractor and accountable management team for experiential and entertainment production — owning scope, schedule, budget, crew, vendors, and technology across all eight phases, from discovery to close, the way a GC owns a build.

Do you provide operations leadership?+

Yes. We provide operations leadership — logistics, workforce and crew management, vendor and procurement coordination, health, safety and compliance, programming, and day-of operations for venues, clubs, stadiums, and hospitality environments.

Do you handle event technology integrations and installs?+

Yes. We implement and integrate event technology — production and resource management (ATLVS), workforce and crew management (COMPVSS), and ticketing and fan engagement (GVTEWAY) — alongside third-party integrations, on-site installs, data, and analytics.

Which industries and verticals do you serve?+

Festivals, concerts and tours, brand activations, immersive experiences, sporting events, TV, film and broadcast, health and wellness, premium hospitality, luxury retail, and water and motorsports — for brands, promoters, venue operators, general contractors, and production directors worldwide.
