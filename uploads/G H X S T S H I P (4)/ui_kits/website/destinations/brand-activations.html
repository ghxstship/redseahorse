Answers

Activations FAQ.

How is GHXSTSHIP different from a traditional agency?+

Three ways: we own the full eight-phase production lifecycle end to end (not just creative), we build our own technology (ATLVS, COMPVSS, GVTEWAY), and Operations — logistics, crew, compliance, and analytics — is a dedicated vertical, not an afterthought.

What does GHXSTSHIP do for brand activations?+

End-to-end experiential marketing: concept and creative, fabrication and scenic, spatial design, interactive technology, permitting, and measurement — on our eight-phase lifecycle.

Who are activations for?+

Brands, agencies, CMOs, and creative directors who need a partner from concept through reporting.

Can you measure activation impact?+

Yes — we build in measurement and reporting so the activation ties back to business outcomes.
